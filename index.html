About The Event


Building on the resounding success of the past edition of ELEKTRA technology summit held in 2018, this year's summit focuses on the advancement in technology and importance of developing skills. As the social impact of technology is being discussed in every nook and corner, there is an urge to bridge the gap between industries an academics. ELEKTRA brings you a collaborative platform for the engineering graduates to analyse and examine the emerging technologies and understand the strategies to balance innovation and methodologies. ELEKTRA 19 is organised by YDYUD in association with IEEE PES SB chapter Munnar. The aim of this initiative is to provide a platform for a genuine exchange of ideas and importantly exchange of knowledge between students and experts.

About YDYUD


Powering up the youth to enhance their knowledge on the basics of sparks and to enrich them to tackle the solutions to fight among the darkness of difficulties with the sword of light. YDYUD aims the students of CEM to deeply analyse and exhibit their thirst for knowledge and quench them up with the best solutions present. Our objective is to provide the students and faculties to work creatively and systematically. YDYUD annually conducts many programs and fests to promote their student's activities and their abilities of acquiring and analyzing various electrical skills based on the emerging technologies. Fests conducted based on the advanced skills results in acquiring updated knowledge among faculties and students so as to effectively survive with the outer industrial skills.

Terms and Conditions

1.Decision made by the judges will be final.
2.Authors of accepted papers are invited to register, make payment, attend and present their research paper at one of the conference sessions. Papers which are not presented will not be included in the Book of abstracts or Book of proceedings.
3.The official language of the conference is English and all papers and presentations should be in English.
4.Payment is required for each individual author in multi authors' proceedings if each of the authors wants to attend the conference and receive attendance certificate. Authors of the submitted abstracts for presentation must also make separate registration for the conference by filling in the Registration Form. Only the author who will come to the conference, present the paper and wishes to receive the Attendance Certificate needs to pay for the registration fee. If all the authors want to attend the conference and receive attendance certificate each of them should make separate registration and separately pay for the registration fee.
